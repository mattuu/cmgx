Who we are…

CodeMagix is small software house based in Leeds, West Yorkshire. We specialize in utilzing IT to solve every day business problems our clients face.


Our mission

We will always strive for comprehensive and thought-through solution to your problem, keeping you at centre of our work. We strongly believe there is a right balance between simplicity and elegance, without compromising on quality.

What we do…

Understanding your business model and priorities is the most important part of our job. We will help you to identify and measure your needs, identify any potential gaps in your IT infrastructure, advise you on industry best practices and support you in software implementation.


Bespoke Software Development

With over decade of experience, we can deliver any software, regardless of its size and complexity.


Requirements Analysis

We deliver bespoke solutions to meet your business requirements 100%. We will collaborate with you to improve your specification and provide feasibility study.


Database Development

We cover all aspects of database design and development from T-SQL coding, indexing and performance optimization.


Mentoring & Training

We can offer customised group-based or one-to-one training and mentoring to kick start your SQL Server based projects and develop your team.


IT Architecture

Our extensive knowledge of Microsoft's platform means we are ideally placed to help define flexible data architecture for your business.
